News & Updates

Team March 15, 2026

Season 2025/2026: Official Project Kickoff

20 students gathered for the official launch of our inaugural Formula Student season. Departments are formed, goals are set, and the countdown to competition has begun.

Engineering February 28, 2026

Chassis Concept Design: Three Approaches Evaluated

Our chassis department has completed initial concept evaluation, comparing tubular steel, aluminium hybrid, and monocoque approaches based on weight, cost, and manufacturability.

Partnerships February 10, 2026

Sponsorship Campaign Launches for 2025/2026

Our corporate relations team has kicked off the sponsorship drive, reaching out to local and national companies. We're looking for both financial and in-kind partners.

Engineering January 20, 2026

Motor Selection: EMRAX 228 Electric Motor Chosen

After evaluating multiple options, the powertrain department has selected a EMRAX 228 electric electric motor for our first car, balancing performance, reliability, and cost.

Team January 5, 2026

Recruitment Drive: Join CNFS for 2025/2026

We're recruiting across all departments - from mechanical engineering to business and marketing. If you're a student at Centrale Nantes, there's a place for you.

Milestones December 15, 2025

CNFS Founded: Centrale Nantes Enters Formula Student

Centrale Nantes Formula Student is officially registered as a student association. Our goal: design, build, and race a competition car within our first academic year.
