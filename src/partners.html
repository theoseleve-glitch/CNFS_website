Why Partner With Us

Invest in the Future of Engineering

By partnering with CNFS, you support hands-on engineering education while gaining visibility among 20 future engineers and the wider Centrale Nantes community.

  • ✓ Brand visibility on the car, team uniforms, website, and social media
  • ✓ Recruitment access to top engineering talent from Centrale Nantes
  • ✓ Event presence at Formula Student competitions across Europe
  • ✓ CSR value - support STEM education and student initiatives
  • ✓ Technical collaboration - test your products in a real engineering environment

Beyond Financial Support

In-Kind Partnerships

We also welcome partnerships through donations of materials, components, software licenses, tools, and technical expertise.

In-kind sponsors receive equivalent visibility and benefits based on the value of their contribution. This is an excellent way for companies to showcase their products in a real engineering context while supporting student innovation.

Materials

Steel, aluminium, composites, fasteners, raw materials for chassis and bodywork.

Software

CAD, FEA, CFD, data acquisition, and ECU tuning software licenses.

Components

Engine parts, brakes, bearings, electronics, sensors, tyres, and wheels.

Expertise

Technical mentorship, workshop access, manufacturing support, and training.
