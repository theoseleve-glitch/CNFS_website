Season 2025/2026

Team Milestones

Key dates in our journey from concept to competition.

Sep 15 2026
Team

Season Kickoff & Recruitment

Centrale Nantes Campus

Official launch of the 2025/2026 season. Open recruitment across all departments — mechanical, electrical, software, business, and marketing.

Nov 20 2026
Milestone

Concept Design Review

Engineering Building, Centrale Nantes

All departments present their initial concepts. The team selects the final design direction for chassis, powertrain, suspension, and aerodynamics.

Jan 15 2027
Milestone

Critical Design Review

Centrale Nantes

Final design freeze. All CAD models, FEA simulations, and manufacturing plans are reviewed and approved. Production-ready drawings are released.

Feb 01 2027
Milestone

Manufacturing Begins

University Workshops

Chassis welding, component machining, composite layup, and electronics assembly begin in Centrale Nantes workshops.

Apr 15 2027
Team

Rollout & Shakedown

Centrale Nantes

The car hits the ground for the first time. Systems integration, first fire-up, and initial shakedown testing. A landmark moment for the team.

May 01 2027
Team

Testing & Driver Training

Regional Circuit, Nantes Area

Intensive testing programme: suspension tuning, engine mapping, brake balance, and driver training sessions ahead of competition.
