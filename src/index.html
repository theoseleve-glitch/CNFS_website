Who We Are

Engineering
Excellence

We are Centrale Nantes Formula Student, a student-led team dedicated to designing, building, and racing a single-seat competition car from scratch every year.

20 students from all engineering disciplines, united by a passion for motorsport, innovation, and hands-on learning.

The Competition

What is Formula Student?

The world's largest engineering competition. Design, build, race — judged on engineering, cost, business, and performance.

⚙

Engineering Design

Present and defend every technical decision to a panel of industry engineers.

⏱

Dynamic Events

Acceleration, skidpad, autocross, and a gruelling 22km endurance race on track.

Prove your car is viable — business plan, cost analysis, and manufacturing strategy.

Our Vision

From Concept
to Autonomous

2025/2026

Year 1: Concept Class

Project Falcon. EMRAX 228 electric motor, tubular steel chassis. Enter FS France concept class at Transpolis.

Year 2

Full Electric Competition

Compete with the fully built EV. Refine powertrain, battery management, and on-track performance.

Future

Autonomous — Someday

Driverless category. Sensor fusion, machine learning, real-time autonomous decisions.

🏆

Competition Ready

Our goal: compete at Formula Student France (Transpolis) in Year 1, proving ourselves against established European teams.

🎓

Learning by Doing

Not just a race — an educational platform. Classroom theory meets real-world engineering.

🤝

Industry Partnerships

Lasting relationships with partners who provide funding, materials, software, and expertise.
